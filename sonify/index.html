Sonifying the Multi-Probe Sky

🔊Power Spectrum Sonifier

Turn the CMB, tSZ, CIB and kSZ power spectra into sound. Mix components, choose a survey band, sweep across angular scales and frequencies, play it with instruments, and click an interactive 3D sky to listen to clusters.

ⓘ How it works & what every control does

Things to try inside the Sonifier: 🌟 Big Bang (hear the CMB acoustic peaks), ℓ-Sweep & ν-Sweep, Build-Up (CMB→+tSZ→+CIB), Instruments, Duet (two probes’ cross-correlation as harmony), and stereo & 3D spatial audio. In the Sky Explorer & Globe, try Scan mode and clicking clusters. Headphones recommended.
